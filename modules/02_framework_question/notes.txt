    - Three levels of D3M: 
      - Discrete Decisions: Quick, low-stakes choices requiring minimal data, such as a single data point to confirm a hunch.
      - Operational Decisions: Involve monitoring data over time, typically using dashboards to track performance and make real-time adjustments.
      - Strategic Decisions: High-stakes, long-term decisions requiring comprehensive and contextual data, often involving external data sources to assess risks and opportunities.
